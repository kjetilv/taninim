Lyrics[edit]

Text: Birkot HaShahar/Morning Blessings
Arrangement: Ayelet Rose Gottlieb


You who removes sleep from my eyes, slumber from my eyelids

Great Spirit, open my lips and I will sing your praises
Great Spirit, the soul you have given me is pure.

Thankful am I in your presence, spirit who lives and endures
for you’ve returned my soul to me with compassion.

הַמַּעֲבִירָה שֵׁנָה מֵעֵינַי וּתְנוּמָה מֵעַפְעַפָּי.‏

אֲדֹנָי, שְׂפָתַי תִּפְתָּח; וּפִי, יַגִּיד תְּהִלָּתֶךָ.
